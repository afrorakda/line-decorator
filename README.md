# Line Decorator

Part of the Fridge Combo project. Instantly frame and embellish your text lists with prefix and suffix additions.

## 1 Tool / 1 Action

Just paste your lines. **Line Decorator** adds your specified characters to the beginning and end of every line simultaneously. Perfect for creating bullet points, wrapping text in quotes, or formatting data for code.

## Philosophy
* **1 Tool, 1 Action.**
* **No Login / No Ads / No Tracking**
* **Browser-only Privacy** (Works on low-spec devices)

## How to Use
1. Paste your list of text.
2. Enter your desired **Prefix** (head) and **Suffix** (tail).
3. Click **Decorate & Copy** to see the transformation and copy the result.
